About Us

Welcome to Digital Grandma, a heartfelt project born out of creativity and passion for making a positive impact in the realm of emotional support.


I'm Evelina Ogu, and I'm excited to share the story behind Digital Grandma.




Our Origin:

Digital Grandma is more than just a project; it's a labor of love that originated as part of the Web Authoring module at Kerry College. Fueled by a desire to explore the intersection of technology and emotional well-being, the concept took shape with a vision to offer more than just practical features â€“ to provide genuine companionship and support.

The Vision:

In a world where the pace of life can sometimes feel overwhelming, Digital Grandma was conceived with the vision of redefining the landscape of digital assistance. The goal is to create a space where individuals can turn to a reliable, empathetic companion in moments of need, transcending the limitations of traditional support structures.

Our Commitment:

Driven by a profound empathy for the human experience, the team at Digital Grandma is committed to making a positive impact on users' daily lives. We believe in the transformative power of thoughtful technological solutions, particularly in the realms of artificial intelligence and digital companionship.

What Sets Us Apart:

Digital Grandma goes beyond being a project; it's a testament to the belief that technology can be a force for good, fostering connection and understanding. The platform aims to contribute to the overall mental health and emotional resilience of users, providing comfort and companionship in a digitally connected world.

Join Us on this Journey:

As we continue to evolve and grow, we invite you to join us on this journey. Whether you're here to explore the project's roots at Kerry College or to experience the innovative features of Digital Grandma, we're thrilled to have you with us.

Thank you for being a part of the Digital Grandma story.

Warm regards,

Evelina Ogu and the Digital Grandma Team
